📖 Mission Briefing

The world's deadliest threats don't make the news. They disappear.

The Bureau of Assassination—BOA—operates beyond borders, beyond law, and beyond denial.

When a covert operation deep beneath the earth goes catastrophically wrong, BOA uncovers a weapon without limits. A force powerful enough to rewrite global power.

Helicopters fall. Facilities burn. Classified assets vanish.

The race against time stretches from underground bunkers to the vast expanse of space. The mission has begun. The truth is classified. And nothing stays buried forever.
